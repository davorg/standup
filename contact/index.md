We welcome messages from comedians, producers, venues, publicists and readers.

For review invitations, press releases, interview proposals and other comedy
news, email [hello@standup.co.uk](mailto:hello@standup.co.uk).

Please include as much relevant information as possible, such as the performer
and show name, venue, dates, press material and an official ticket link.

We read every message, but coverage cannot be guaranteed.
