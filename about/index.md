StandUp.co.uk is an independent publication covering stand-up comedy in the UK,
with a particular interest in the Edinburgh Festival Fringe. There is no
shadowy media collective behind the site: it is written and edited by the
named people on our [team page](/team/).

Dave Cross registered the domain in 1999. After years of sitting on some very
grand plans for it, he revived the site as a home for comedy news, previews,
reviews and recommendations.

## Editorial policy

### Independence

Our editorial decisions are our own. An invitation, press release, interview
offer or complimentary ticket does not guarantee coverage. It certainly does
not guarantee positive coverage.

Where a writer receives a complimentary ticket, we disclose that clearly in
the resulting article or review.

### Conflicts of interest

Writers should not cover performers, productions or organisations where a
personal or professional relationship could compromise their independence. If
a potential conflict is unavoidable but coverage remains worthwhile, we will
declare it clearly.

### Use of artificial intelligence

We use AI tools to assist with research, drafting and artwork. A named human
writer or editor reviews and approves everything we publish and remains
responsible for its accuracy, fairness and editorial judgement.

### Corrections

We correct factual errors as soon as reasonably possible. Substantive
corrections are noted on the article, including the date and a brief
explanation of what changed. Minor spelling, grammar and formatting fixes may
be made without a separate note.

If you believe something needs correcting, please email
[hello@standup.co.uk](mailto:hello@standup.co.uk) or use our
[contact page](/contact/).
